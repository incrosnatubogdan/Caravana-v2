Caravan 78 - Motoșeni, Bacău

"In Motoșeni I had the feeling that all the sacrifices we make to become doctors have a purpose. I'm happy to have been able to help with a simple EKG or a piece of advice. The Caravan, seen through the eyes of a second year med student, is not only a volunteering opportunity but also a close group of doctors and students who do their best for a small community where access to medical services is poor."

Mălina Epure, Caravana cu Medici Iași volunteer
