Caravana 46 - Corbasca, județul Bacău

It was a weekend with most heart touching sceneries of gratefulness on the one hand side and with emotional displeasure on the other side from those who didn’t have the chance to be examined. At the same time, it was a thoughtful experience by feeling the adverse consequences for those, to which the national health care system is out of reach. The diagnosed findings clearly confirmed the need and importance for a better primary health and medical care in the deprived rural areas.I was most impressed by the respect, compassion and personal devotion offered by the entire team of young committed doctors and by the most valuable health care services which they offered to the rural community of Corbasca – it deserves my utmost respect!

Ronnie Bühr, President of Mana in Mana Foundation, Switzerland"
